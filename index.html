美国国际教育发展基金会

Education Advancement Fund International

教育促进国际基金会(EAFI)是一家非营利性组织(NGO),总部位于檀香山,由夏威夷大学政治学教授周晓(Kate Zhou)指导。 EAFI的主要目标是通过教育和培训,帮助弱势群体突破社会和政治对其个人自主权的限制，从而获得赋能。

The Education Advancement Foundation International (EAFI) is a non-profit organization (NGO) headquartered in Honolulu and directed by University of Hawaii political science professor Kate Zhou. EAFI's primary goal is to empower disadvantaged groups by helping them overcome social and political constraints on their personal autonomy through education and training.

致学生的一封真诚的信

A Heartfelt Letter to a Student

新衣终会旧损破敝，被弃置一旁；一餐不过是一时一刻的温饱。但你拥有的，是持续学习的机会、直面世界的底气，以及懂得珍爱他人的心。

强者视挑战为机遇，奋勇前行；弱者视困境为绝路，沉溺自怜。

"New clothes will eventually wear out, tear, and be cast aside; a meal is just that—a single meal at a single moment. But what you have is the opportunity to keep learning, the confidence to face the world, and a heart that knows how to cherish and love others.

The strong see challenges as opportunities and press forward courageously; the weak see them as dead ends and wallow in self-pity."

韧性之力：论坚持的品格

Resilience and the Power of Perseverance

收到你的回信，我们深受触动。字里行间透出的希望与决心，让我们感到所有的努力终于有了回响。

你知道我最敬佩什么样的人吗？是那些逆流而上的勇者，那些以不屈意志对抗命运的人，那些咬紧牙关把梦想化作垫脚石的行者。他们眼中有光，心怀天下却脚踏实地。而事实上——他们就是你。他们就是我们。

"We were deeply moved to receive your reply. Seeing the hope and determination shining through your letter made us feel that everyone’s efforts have finally borne some fruit.

Do you know who I admire most? Those who rise against the odds, those who defy fate with unyielding resolve, those who grit their teeth and turn dreams into stepping stones. They are the ones with vision, the ones who carry the world in their hearts while staying grounded in action. And in truth—they are you. They are us."

普惠教育的真谛

The Value of Accessible Education

我们全家就靠爸爸打零工维持生计。每到新学期临近，父母都为我的学费发愁，我也跟着难过——生怕就这样失去继续读书的机会。

我的成绩在班里一直名列前茅。一到三年级担任学习委员，现在四年级是班长。上学期语文95分，数学97分，但和城里学校的同学比，还差得很远。

"Our daily expenses rely entirely on Dad doing odd jobs to make ends meet. Every time the new school term approaches, my parents worry about where to get the money for my tuition, and it makes me sad too—I’m afraid I might lose the chance to continue my education.

My academic performance is among the top in my class. From first to third grade, I served as the class study monitor, and now in fourth grade, I’m the class president. Last semester, I scored 95 in Chinese and 97 in math, but compared to students from other schools, I still have a long way to go."

计划活动

Program Activities

该组织成立于2002年，最初主要专注于湘西少数民族自治州（中国湖南省西部，以苗族和土家族为主）以及云南省（包含纳西族、藏族等25个以上少数民族）。过去十年来，EAFI已在中国及亚洲其他地区拓展了新项目。

The organization was founded in 2002 and initially focused primarily on the Xiangxi Minority Autonomous Prefecture (located in western Hunan Province, China, with a majority of Miao and Tujia ethnic groups) and Yunnan Province (home to over 25 ethnic minorities including the Naxi and Tibetan peoples). Over the past decade, EAFI has expanded its programs to new areas across China and other parts of Asia.
